The WAR Series

Triumph! is a fast-play set of rules for miniature wargaming. Covering the ancient and medieval periods, Triumph! allows you to recreate the armies of Ramesses and Muwatalli, Caesar and Pompey, Saladin and Richard the Lionheart.

We'll be running a wide range of demonstration games at Cold Wars 2017. Stop by and give it a try!

Meshwesh is the online database for Triumph army lists. Fully searchable, Meshwesh makes it easy to explore the armies of history.
