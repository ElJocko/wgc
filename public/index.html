Triumph! is a fast-play set of rules for miniature wargaming. Covering the ancient and medieval periods, Triumph! allows you to recreate the armies of Ramesses and Muwatalli, Caesar and Pompey, Saladin and Richard the Lionheart.

Battle Cards: Pre-Final PDF

Triumph! battle cards add depth and history to a battle of Triumph! through special rules and events that are specific to particular armies. At the same time, they’re designed to provide this extra flavor without throwing off the balance of the game and without overshadowing the underlying game engine.

The Pre-Final version of the battle card rules is now available. This version has the complete text of the final rules, but doesn't include the final formatting and artwork.

Triumph! Campaign Book, Volume One

First Look: Caesar's Gallic Wars

The Washington Grand Company has released a first look at our upcoming campaign book. This campaign book teaser includes a single campaign (Caesar’s Gallic Wars) and provides a preview of the format that the campaign books will use as we move forward. The complete campaign book, slated for release in 2021, will include seven campaigns from history:

  • Bronze Age Collapse (Sea Peoples Invasion and the Collapse of 1200 BC)
  • Viking Fury (Viking Raiders and the British Isles)
  • Caesar's Gallic Wars (Roman Conquest of Gaul, 58 BC)
  • Fourth War of the Diadachoi (Wars of Alexander's Successors, 308-301 BC)
  • Mittelalterliches Deutschland (High Medieval Germany, 1425 AD)
  • God Wills It! (First Crusade, 1097-1120 AD)
  • Mandate of Heaven (Warring States China, 260 BC)
